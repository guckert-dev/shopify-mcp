Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Shopify MCP Server ("we", "our", or "the connector") handles information when you use it to connect Claude to your Shopify store.

Summary: Your credentials stay on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your data to third parties. All communication is directly between your computer and your Shopify store.

1. Information We Access

When you use the Shopify MCP Server, it accesses your Shopify store data through the Shopify Admin API, including:

  • Products, collections, and inventory
  • Orders, fulfillments, and refunds
  • Customers and their order history
  • Discounts and marketing campaigns
  • Store settings and configuration

This data is accessed only when you actively use Claude to interact with your store.

2. How Your Data is Handled

Local Storage Only: Your Shopify store URL and Admin API access token are stored locally on your device in the Claude Desktop configuration file. We never have access to these credentials.

Direct Communication: All API requests go directly from your computer to Shopify's servers. No data passes through any intermediary servers we operate.

No Data Collection: We do not collect, log, or store any of your Shopify store data. We do not use analytics or tracking.

3. Third-Party Services

The connector interacts with:

  • Shopify: Your data is subject to Shopify's Privacy Policy
  • Claude/Anthropic: Your conversations are subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy

4. Data Security

We implement security best practices:

  • All API communication uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Credentials are stored locally and never transmitted to us
  • We recommend using API tokens with minimal required scopes
  • The connector is open source for transparency

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Remove the connector at any time to revoke access
  • Revoke the API token in your Shopify admin to immediately disable access
  • Review our open source code to verify our practices

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the connector evolves. Significant changes will be communicated through our GitHub repository.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions or concerns:

  • Email: security@lemay.app
  • GitHub: Open an issue
